Size of sea urchins depends on the species. They usually have 1.2 to 3.9 inches in diameter.
Sea urchins have globe-like shape of the body that is covered with large number of long spines. 
Bony plates form shell that provides protection for the soft inner parts. Red sea urchin has the longest lifespan on Earth. 
It can survive up to 200 years in the wild.
